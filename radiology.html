Radiology Program at City College

City College is proud to offer a Radiology program designed to prepare students for rewarding careers in medical imaging and diagnostic technology. This program combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical training to ensure graduates are well-equipped to meet the demands of the healthcare industry. The program runs for three years, starting at Level 4, advancing to Level 5, and culminating at Level 6, with each level comprising two semesters.

Program Structure

  • Academic Calendar: Classes commence in October and conclude in August, with rigorous end-of-semester examinations ensuring students’ academic excellence.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: The program emphasizes both the scientific principles and practical applications of radiology, offering a balanced learning experience that prepares students for the real-world healthcare environment.

Why Choose Our Radiology Program?

1. Expert Faculty

Our highly qualified and experienced instructors bring a wealth of knowledge and practical expertise to the classroom. Their dedication ensures that every student receives a world-class education tailored to their needs.

2. Cutting-Edge Training

The program includes intensive training in modern imaging techniques such as X-rays, CT scans, and MRI. Students gain hands-on experience in state-of-the-art laboratories, enhancing their technical skills and confidence.

3. Career-Ready Graduates

Radiology is a vital component of modern medicine, and our program ensures graduates are ready to excel in roles such as radiologic technologists, imaging specialists, or further their studies in advanced medical imaging fields.

4. Supportive Learning Environment

City College creates a welcoming and motivating environment where students can thrive academically and professionally. Our personalized approach to education ensures every student’s success.

Additional Benefits

  • Advanced Facilities: Our radiology labs are equipped with cutting-edge diagnostic imaging tools, providing students with practical experience that mirrors professional settings.
  • Active Student Engagement: From the moment they enroll, students are immersed in academic and extracurricular activities that prepare them for the challenges of the healthcare industry.
  • Affordable Quality Education: At City College, we believe in delivering top-tier education at an accessible cost, making it an excellent investment in your future.

Enroll Today

By joining the Radiology program at City College, students embark on a journey toward a fulfilling career in healthcare. Parents and guardians can trust that their children will receive a top-notch education in a safe, professional, and nurturing environment.
